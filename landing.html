How it works

From request to donation
in minutes

No middlemen. No waiting rooms. Hospitals find donors, connect on WhatsApp, and save lives — all through one platform.

01

Hospital submits a request

Fill in patient details, blood type, urgency level, and component type using the NBS-aligned form.

02

Donors matched instantly

All compatible active donors nearby surface — sorted by proximity and blood type compatibility, no cap on results.

03

Contact via WhatsApp

One tap opens a WhatsApp chat directly with the donor. Real conversations, right away.

04

Donation confirmed

Hospital marks it complete. Donor's record updates automatically. Donor-only confirmations are flagged.

05

Lives saved across Ghana

Every donation is logged. Every donor builds a verified giving record. Every hospital builds a reliable blood network.

06

Donor recognition

Committed donors are recognised with a certificate of honour at 10 successful confirmed donations.

Legal compliance & ethical standards

Blood Connector operates under Ghana's Health Facilities Regulation Authority (HeFRA), the National Blood Service Act, Data Protection Act 2012 (Act 843), and the Criminal Offences Act. Blood is not a commodity. All donations are strictly voluntary. Any misuse of this platform — including solicitation of payment for blood — is a criminal offence. All users must accept our compliance declaration before accessing the platform.
